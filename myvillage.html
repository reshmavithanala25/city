Welcome to Hyderabad

Hyderabad, the capital of Telangana, India, is a historic city known as the "City of Pearls" for its diamond and pearl trade. It blends rich Islamic and Hindu cultures with a booming IT sector, making it a hub for technology, film (Tollywood), and diverse cuisine, including the famous Hyderabadi Biryani.

City Foundation

Hyderabad was founded in 1591, planned around the Charminar at its center.

Urban Life

Hyderabad is a vibrant city combining historical landmarks with modern infrastructure, serving as the political, cultural, and economic hub of Telangana.

Parks & Nature

Major parks like Indira Park and Lumbini Park provide green spaces and recreational areas. Nehru Zoological Park and other eco-projects highlight the city’s environmental efforts.

Food

Hyderabad cuisine blends Mughlai, Turkic, Arabic, and local flavors. Famous dishes include Hyderabadi Biryani, Haleem, Osmania Biscuits, and Qubani ka Meetha.

Education

Hyderabad offers top educational institutions like Osmania University, University of Hyderabad, and numerous schools and colleges.

Temples

Key temples include Birla Mandir and Chilkur Balaji Temple, reflecting the city’s rich spiritual and architectural heritage.
