    • Festivals of Hyderabad

    Bonalu

    A traditional Telangana festival dedicated to Goddess Mahakali, celebrated with great devotion.

    Bathukamma

    A colorful floral festival celebrated by women, symbolizing Telangana culture and traditions.

    Ramzan (Eid-ul-Fitr)

    Celebrated after the holy month of fasting, Hyderabad is famous for its festive food and prayers.

    Diwali

    The festival of lights celebrated across the city with lamps, fireworks, and sweets.

    Ganesh Chaturthi

    Huge Ganesh idols are installed and immersed in Hussain Sagar with grand processions.
