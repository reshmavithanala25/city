Famous Temples in Hyderabad

Birla Mandir

Birla Mandir is a beautiful white marble temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, located on Naubat Pahad with a stunning view of the city.

Chilkur Balaji Temple

Popularly known as Visa Balaji Temple, devotees visit here seeking blessings for overseas travel and success.

Jagannath Temple

Built in Odia architectural style, this temple is dedicated to Lord Jagannath and is famous during Rath Yatra.
